About Me

My name is Iman Gareeboo. I am a doctoral student in the University of Miami's English Department. I research Jane Austen's novels, her contemporaries, and adaptations of Austen's work, as well as the relationships between Austen adaptations.

My family is originally from Mauritius, with South Asian and Asian ancestry. I was born in England, was raised mostly in Texas and Florida in the USA, and have lived in Mauritius, England, and France.
